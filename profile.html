Meet the Artist

The hands and heart behind Happy Hands Hideaway

Kandice

I’m a mixed media artist who loves turning clay, upscaled materials, and natural treasures into whimsical, joyful creations.

My work is inspired by the world around us — the beauty of nature, the magic hidden in everyday moments, and the carefree curiosity of childhood.

I love crafting pieces that make people smile, spark a sense of wonder, and invite them to explore a little world of imagination.

Each sculpture, trinket, or mixed-media creation is my way of celebrating playfulness, joy, and the simple pleasures that surround us.
